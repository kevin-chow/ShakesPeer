A Midsummer Night's Dream
1595, Comedy
Plot Summary [ {{showSummary ? 'Hide' : 'Show'}} ]
A Midsummer Night's Dream portrays the events surrounding the marriage of Theseus, the Duke of Athens, to Hippolyta (the former queen of the Amazons). These include the adventures of four young Athenian lovers and a group of six amateur actors (the mechanicals) who are controlled and manipulated by the fairies who inhabit the forest in which most of the play is set. (Wikipedia)
